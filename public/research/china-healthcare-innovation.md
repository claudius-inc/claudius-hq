## Executive Summary

China's healthcare sector is undergoing a profound transformation from generic manufacturing and low-cost distribution toward genuine biopharmaceutical innovation. This report consolidates research across 4 sub-sectors (18 companies) representing the key beneficiaries of this structural shift.

### Investment Thesis

**Three converging tailwinds:**
1. **Policy Support** — Streamlined drug approvals (review time cut from 4.5 years to ~1 year), R&D incentives, BMI expansion
2. **Silver Economy** — 65+ population to reach 27% by 2040; healthcare spend catching up from 5.4% to DM-peer 10% of GDP
3. **Global Recognition** — Record licensing-out deals ($50B+ in 2025), MNC collaborations validating China R&D capabilities

**Key catalyst:** Biosecure Act final version significantly watered down — no companies named, 5-year safe harbor, narrowed scope.
